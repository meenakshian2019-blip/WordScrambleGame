Scramble-Snap is a modern, high-speed word scramble game built with a premium glassmorphic interface. Challenge your vocabulary across 12 unique categories and 5 levels of increasing difficulty.

Features
- 12 Themes: Sports, Technology, Space, Nature, Food, Travel, Music, Movies, Fashion, Business, Gaming, and Education.
- Progressive Difficulty: Unlock new levels by completing the previous ones.
- Dynamic Timer: The time limit decreases as you advance to higher levels.
- Smart Reveal: If time runs out, the correct answer is shown before moving to the next word.
- Hint System: Stuck? Reveal the first and last letters of the word.
- Glassmorphism UI: A sleek, dark-themed design with smooth animations and transitions.

How to Play
1. Select a Theme from the menu.
2. Click on an unlocked Level to start.
3. Unscramble the word shown on the screen and type your answer.
4. Press Submit or hit Enter to check your answer.
5. Complete all 5 words in a level to unlock the next one.

Controls
- Keyboard: Type your answer and press Enter to submit.
- Mouse/Touch: Use the on-screen buttons for Submitting, Hints, and Navigation.

Project Structure
- index.html - The structure of the game screens.
- style.css - The visual design and animations.
- script.js - The game logic and word data.
